20th Regiment of Foot

Gaming, Community, Competition

Enhanced Gaming Experience
Join our Group

The 20th Regiment of Foot is a global organization that promotes online gaming through quality events, servers, and services for both the amateur and professional player base and community.

Founded on February 28th, 2014, our roots graciously grew from Mount and Blade Warband, but we eventually branched out towards establishing organized groups accross multiple games. We strive to provide both quality casual and competitive experiences for our members — achieved through years of experience, strong administrative foundations, and a robust staff structure.

Since 2014, we’ve organized thousands of unique events, hosted professional competitive matches, raised money for various charities, and as of 2020, employ over 20 staff members. With the continued growth of the 20th Regiment of Foot, we intend to bring the same incredible events to our incredible community and the related public.

The 20th Regiment of Foot
Community

The 20th regiment of Foot provides some of the most optimized, performant game servers, enabling communities to play to the best of their abilities via the highest server-side performance possible. Offering such infrastructure, publically and without charge, to our gaming communities is an obligation we proudly hold.

Visit our Technology & Infrastructure page for a white-page and additional information regarding our sever environment(s).

Playing with the public communities is another important focus for the 20th Regiment of Foot — especially regarding recruitment. Our recruitment goal is to provide quality over quantity. Understanding that quality is invested and produced, our members have always shown willingness and proven their efficacy to ensure the best communications and sociability.
